    Money Hub

    The Money Hub show concept was birthed in Johannesburg, South Africa by Sandra Appertey with the theme “Understand and grow your money the right way” with the core aim to guide our African youth to be financially responsible, independent and to make good use of existing and emerging opportunities for a successful life and was aired on Soweto TV. The show currently airs in Accra, Ghana on Happy FM, Y FM and ETV Ghana and is produced by Eagles Netwox.

    At Money Hub one of our philosophies is education. It is our belief that informed populations make better decisions. We realize how important financial inclusion is to you, your family and your future and it is our aim is to assist our audience to protect their wealth and create long-term prosperity. The show explores the various Banks, Investment companies, Microfinance institutions, Investment institutions, Telecommunication companies, Institutions that deal with money related gadgets etc. It showcases the various financial institutions and how relevant they are to the ordinary Ghanaian and Africa as a whole.  

    It involves live financial coaching for individuals and businesses that express interest in certain areas thereby providing an opportunity to expose businesses to their target market and vice versa. Here are two categories of the Money Hub show;

    The "Moneyhub Business Summit" aims to provide a platform that bring together businesses, business owners, entrepreneurs and resource persons to engage, interact and network. Speakers are accomplished Business Professionals in Ghana, Germany and South Africa.

    The "MoneyHub Campus Tour" is a financial literacy program specially designed to reorient students on how to make sound, progressive and prudent financial decisions. The program, in collaboration with the SRC of tertiary institutions, is in the form of a 1-day seminar and practical workshop facilitated by seasoned financial experts and life-coaches.

    The show is hosted by Sandra Appertey also known as Casandra (Stage Name) an award-winning gospel musician who has become a household name in both the media and music scene in Ghana and abroad. Sandra has 5 years’ experience in media as a producer, having produced Xpose Afrique (aired in Ghana and SA) and Money hub, aired on Soweto TV, SA, for which she was also the host.

    She’s currently a Co-host of Max Morning Show on MAX TV and Host of Max Keteke show on Max 89.7fm. Money Hub airs on Happy Fm on Wednesdays at 6.15pm, Y 107.9 Fm on Saturdays 3pm and Etv on Mondays at 7.30pm.
